# Privacy Policy - Kreno SaaS Platform

Last Updated: 2026-04-16

## 1. Introduction

Kreno ("we", "us", "our", or "Company") operates the Kreno SaaS platform (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- Full name and email address
- Phone number (for notifications)
- Salon name and location
- Business registration information
- Payment information (processed by Stripe)

### 2.2 Booking Data
- Customer names and contact information
- Appointment dates, times, and service types
- Service preferences and notes

### 2.3 Staff Information
- Employee name, email, and phone number
- Job title and assigned services
- Work schedule and availability

### 2.4 Analytics Data
- Page views and feature usage patterns
- Device type, operating system, and browser information
- IP address (for rate limiting and fraud prevention)

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use data to:
- Provide the Service (bookings, payments, notifications)
- Improve the Service (analytics, bug fixes)
- Contact you about your account
- Detect and prevent fraud

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data with:
- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **Resend**: Email delivery
- **Twilio**: SMS notifications (optional)
- **MongoDB Atlas**: Database hosting
- **Render.com**: Server hosting

## 5. Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data kept while account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data purged within 30 days
- Backups: Kept for up to 7 days
- Server logs: Retained for 30 days

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 Right to Access
Request a copy of your data at privacy@kreno.ch

### 6.2 Right to Rectification
Update your profile directly or contact support

### 6.3 Right to Erasure
Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)

### 6.4 Right to Data Portability
Request your data in JSON or CSV format

## 7. Security

We implement:
- HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
- MongoDB Atlas encryption for data at rest
- Rate limiting to prevent brute-force attacks
- Input validation and output encoding
- Regular security audits

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Our servers are hosted in the United States. By using Kreno, you consent to this transfer.

## 10. GDPR Compliance

For EU users:
- We process data based on your consent and contract performance
- We have data processing agreements with third-party vendors
- You have the rights listed in Section 6 above
- Contact privacy@kreno.ch with questions

## 11. CCPA Compliance

For California residents:
- You have the right to know what personal information is collected
- You have the right to delete personal information
- You have the right to opt-out of data sharing (though we do not sell data)

## 12. Cookies

We use:
- Session cookies (necessary for authentication)
- Preference cookies (store your settings)
- Analytics cookies (optional, for service improvement)

## 13. Third-Party Links

Kreno may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

## 14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes.

## 15. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy:

**Email**: privacy@kreno.ch
**Support**: support@kreno.ch

For data subject requests (access, deletion, portability), email privacy@kreno.ch and we will respond within 30 days.
